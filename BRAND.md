## Tagline

**Primary (EN):** From France to the World — Tires That Keep Moving

**Primary (FR):** De la France au monde — Des pneus qui continuent leur route

**Short version (bilingual use):** Sourced in France. Delivered worldwide.

> **Why this tagline:** It signals the export dimension immediately, anchors credibility in France (European standard = trust for African and Eastern European buyers), and the "keep moving" double meaning ties the tire product to forward momentum. It works in both languages without losing impact.

## Visual Feel

### The One-Sentence Brief
> Serious European supplier meets emerging market opportunity — clear, traceable, no-nonsense.

### Mood
SMAC Pneu operates in a market where trust is everything. Buyers in Senegal or Poland are wiring money internationally for containers of goods they haven't seen. The visual language must communicate **reliability before aesthetics**. This is not a startup, not a marketplace, not a sustainability brand. It is a trading company that delivers what it promises.

### What it looks like

**Clean over clever.** No gradients, no abstract shapes, no motion graphics. Every element earns its place by conveying information or building trust.

**Industrial precision.** Spacing is generous and deliberate. Grids are tight. Tables are used where they belong — to compare grades, list countries, structure process steps. The site should feel like a well-made PDF that someone actually reads.

**Green as signal, not decoration.** The forest green (`#0F6E56`) is used sparingly — CTAs, section titles, grade highlights, checkmarks. It should feel meaningful when it appears, not wallpapered across every block.

**Bilingual by default.** FR and EN are equals. Language toggle is always visible. No element is an afterthought in either language. Typography must hold at both FR (longer words) and EN (shorter, punchier).

### What it does NOT look like

- ✕ Eco-startup greenwashing (no leaf illustrations, no carbon-offset messaging)
- ✕ Marketplace / e-commerce (no product cards with prices, no cart, no ratings)
- ✕ Corporate grey (no blue gradients, no stock handshake photos)
- ✕ African aesthetic clichés (no maps, no flag emojis as design elements)

### Reference Points (feel, not copy)

- **Contentsquare.com** — B2B SaaS, clean, trustworthy, green palette
- **Maersk.com** — shipping / logistics, structured, global, serious
- **Phenix.com** — circular economy, French B2B, green without being preachy

## Logo Usage

- Minimum size: 32px height
- Clear space: equal to the height of the "S" in SMAC on all sides
- On dark backgrounds (footer, sticky bar): white version
- On light backgrounds: forest green `#0F6E56` version
- Never place on a busy background
- Never recolor the leaf element independently from the tire

## Voice & Tone

| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| Hero / headline | Direct, confident, international |
| Feature descriptions | Precise, factual, no superlatives |
| RFQ form | Warm but efficient — reduce friction |
| Trust signals | Understated — let facts speak |
| Error messages | Clear, helpful, never technical |

**Words to use:** reliable, traceable, compliant, volume, sourced, documented, consistent

**Words to avoid:** innovative, revolutionize, eco-friendly, best-in-class, seamless, cutting-edge
